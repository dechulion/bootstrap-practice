Welcome!

Kick off your shoes, leave the bustle of the city behind and come join us in our world. A world full of animals, plants, bugs and dirt. A place where all those things work together to create the most efficient systems on the planet.

Those systems are what interest us. At Keron Permaculture Farm, we take advantage of and mimic biological systems that have had millions of years to evolve to produce truly sustainable and organic food, cleaners, medicinals and many other products. Feel free to browse around and get a feel for what we are all about.

About Us

We believe that a healthy body, mind and soul all start in the dirt. The soil is where everything starts from and where everything ends. If we abuse the dirt, polluting it, scraping it up and throwing it around, or just tossing it into the wind, we're ultimately abusing ourselves. OK, that sounds like a lot of hippey-dippey. What we really mean is, we believe there's a better way of growing and living than what the purely industrialized world has to offer. And the best part is, the methods we use are nothing new. They've been here for millions of years. All we have to do is observe and learn more.
